Story

"INSaNE | A Broken Hero" follows the journey of Ashnage Narnesfugen, a young adult who finds himself at a crossroads in life. After graduating from college, Ashnage embarks on a quest to find his purpose and a job in his dream city.

However, his aspirations are abruptly disrupted when an unexpected alien invasion threatens the world. In a twist of fate, a select group of young individuals from all corners of the globe, including Ashnage, are bestowed with mysterious powers. Ashnage discovers his newfound ability to control gravity, granting him immense control over the forces that bind the universe.

Realizing the gravity of the situation, Ashnage joins forces with his fellow empowered friends, forming an unlikely alliance. United by their extraordinary abilities, they set out on a mission to confront and repel the alien invaders, defending humanity from imminent destruction.

As Ashnage and his friends face numerous challenges and battles against otherworldly adversaries, they must also grapple with their own personal struggles and doubts. Ashnage, in particular, experiences inner turmoil as he questions his own capabilities and wrestles with the weight of responsibility placed upon him as a hero.

Throughout their perilous journey, Ashnage and his comrades forge deep bonds, relying on each other's strengths and overcoming their individual weaknesses. Together, they unravel the mysteries behind the alien invasion, uncovering a web of secrets that extend far beyond Earth.

Dedicated to:

All those who have not yet found what they yearn for without knowing what it is, to the broken and the lonely: In this story, you'll may find an answer to fulfill your inner void, get hope and a reason to keep going on through life.
