Story

Ashnage is a young adult looking for a purpose in life, after graduating from college, travels to his dream city But he didn't expected that moving out and find a decent job could be so hard, until something unexpected happened. An alien invasion arrived and the world is in inminent danger, a bunch of youngsters all over the world obtained mysterious powers, Ashnage acquired the power of gravity control, with his new power and new friends, they'll stop the alien invasion.


Dedicated to:

All those who have not yet found what they yearn for without knowing what it is, to the broken and the lonely: In this story, you'll may find an answer to fulfill your inner void, get hope and a reason to keep going on through life.
